Palm Drive Healthcare District's 2014 municipal bankruptcy filing marks the 12th time a California healthcare district has filed for bankruptcy since 1995. The Association of California Healthcare Districts lists 78 such entities. Below, I have mashed up ACHD's healthcare district map with 2013 special district financial data supplied by the California State Controller's Office on its new open data website, ByTheNumbers. In addition, I provide selected data from district financial statements, where available.

When the value of a local government's liabilities exceeds its assets, that entity faces a heightened risk of bankruptcy. The Google Fusion map denotes such "negative equity" situations by displaying the district in red. Green districts have positive equity, while I was unable to find equity data in the SCO database for those colored black. You can display selected financial highlights as well as links to audited financial statements by clicking on a district polygon. More financial details are available from the "row" view of the Google Fusion table.
